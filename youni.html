OVERVIEW

Youni is a French startup that aims to improve the amount of information prospective exchange students have about universities around the world. As a UX/UI intern in spring 2015, I proposed a redesign of the homepage that provided more guidance to users seeking to upload reviews, which the startup had identified as an important goal.

Usability analysis

I began by getting familiar with the homepage and then conducting some informal user interviews with classmates.

I analyzed how the layout of the homepage's features encouranged or hindered user interaction and asked leaders of the tech and business teams what they intended for users to get out of the website.

Advocating for the user, I echoed the concerns and confusion of those I had interviewed.
